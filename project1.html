Project 1: Modern Wisdom Newsletter

Description: Modern Wisdom is a weekly email newsletter that shares insights, thought-provoking ideas, and practical advice for personal growth and self-improvement. The project includes an automated workflow for managing subscriptions and delivering welcome messages to new subscribers.

How It Works:

  • Sign-Up: I created a Google Form for easy sign-up, where subscribers can provide their email addresses.
  • Subscriber Management: The entries are stored in a Google Spreadsheet, automatically updating as new people sign up.
  • Welcome Email: Using Zapier, I set up automation that sends a personalized welcome email to each new subscriber right after they sign up.
  • Weekly Content: Every Monday, I write and format new content using Visual Studio Code. I then paste it directly into Gmail to ensure consistent formatting and an engaging layout.

Skills Demonstrated:

  • Automated workflows with Google Sheets and Zapier
  • Weekly content creation and copywriting
  • Proficiency with Google Forms and Gmail for effective email marketing
